# Competitive Comparison

SveltyCMS is designed for developers who prioritize performance, security, and accessibility. By using Svelte 5 (Runes) and a database-agnostic architecture, we offer a highly competitive alternative to traditional React-based or GUI-heavy CMS platforms.

## Technical Advantages

### 1. Concurrent Throughput & Scalability

In real-world production scenarios, SveltyCMS excels at handling simultaneous traffic. Our authenticated, tenant-aware GraphQL stress tests sustain **over 1,100 requests per second** at 120 users on standard Linux hardware.

While competitors have made significant strides in performance—with some 2026 third-party estimates placing them around 900-1,200 RPS—SveltyCMS **performs competitively at the high end** due to its lack of VDOM overhead and efficient state management.

### 2. Svelte 5 & Zero-Runtime Overhead

The architectural choice of **Svelte 5 Runes** provides fundamental advantages:

- **No Hydration Tax**: SveltyCMS minimizes the JavaScript sent to the client, leading to faster Time-to-Interactive.
- **Fine-Grained Reactivity**: State updates are surgical, avoiding the "re-rendering everything" problem common in React/Vue platforms.
- **Small Footprint**: Our core build remains highly optimized at ~800 KB (Brotli), making it ideal for edge deployments.

### 3. Native Enterprise Security

SveltyCMS treats security as a core feature, not a plugin:

- **Crypto-Chained Audit Logs**: Tamper-evident logs (SHA-256) are standard, ensuring compliance with SOC 2 and GDPR.
- **SCIM 2.0 & SAML**: Native support for automated user provisioning and enterprise SSO.
- **Multi-Tenancy**: Native `tenantId` isolation at the database adapter level, preventing cross-tenant data leaks.

### 4. Accessibility Foundation

We prioritize accessibility as a functional requirement. Our UI components are built on a strong **WCAG 2.2 AA+** foundation, and we are actively adopting **WCAG 3.0 Functional Performance** patterns as the standard evolves.

## Competitive Context

### Payload CMS

Payload is a mature and powerful ecosystem, particularly with its v3 Next.js integration. It offers excellent developer experience and a robust cloud offering. SveltyCMS positions itself as a lighter, faster alternative for teams who prefer the Svelte ecosystem and require absolute performance at the edge.

### Strapi & Directus

Strapi and Directus excel at GUI-first content modeling and have large plugin marketplaces. SveltyCMS is a better fit for developers who prefer a **code-centric** approach with bi-directional GUI sync and sub-millisecond core logic.
